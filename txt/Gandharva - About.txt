Gandharva Wellness, where heightened awakening of all seven senses allows for inner reflection, cleansing, and equilibrium, the road toward creating harmony between nature and the self .  The yogis refer to this as the consciousness of one's surroundings .  These traditional practises have been preserved into the modern era at Gandharvas. At its opulent retreat, Gandharva Wellness where these customs invites you to rebalance the way it all originated.

Gandharva is used to describe a certain mental quality connected to sattva - the purity and wisdom. This characteristic shows up in love of poetry, dancing, and singing. Given that they were also regarded as healers, Gandharvas are said to have incredible healing abilities. That's what Gandharva Wellness is all about, a holistic approach to healing, not just physical and psychological but emotional as well.
